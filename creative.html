Electronica club

This is a club where students can create their own designs for electronic, robotic and embedded systems. The Electronica Club meets every Wednesday between 2:30-4:30pm at digital labs(room no 315) this club will help the students in clearing their basic concepts about electronic devices and circuits.

Example projects

  • cell phone detector
  • water level controller using 8051 microcontroller
  • password based circuit breaker
  • water level indicator
  • mobile jammer circuit
    • and many more
